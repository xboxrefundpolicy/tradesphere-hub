❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

📦 What is Tradesphere Hub? ▼

Tradesphere Hub is an all‑in‑one platform for managing TikTok log delivery, client accounts, orders, gifts, referrals, and more — all from one secure dashboard.

🔑 How do I access my logs? ▼

You will receive a unique link from your admin. Click the link, enter your password, and you'll see your logs instantly.

🎁 How do I redeem a gift? ▼

When you receive a gift link, click it and follow the instructions. If you have a gift code, enter it in the Gift Hub.

📋 What is the waiting list? ▼

If we're out of stock, you can join the waiting list. We'll notify you the moment new logs become available.

🛡️ Is my data secure? ▼

Yes. All data is stored locally in your browser or on our secure servers (depending on the service). Your login is password‑protected.

📧 How can I contact support? ▼

You can reach us via our WhatsApp group or send an email to the admin address shown in your dashboard.
